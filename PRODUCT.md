# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Hearing and deaf individuals in Thailand looking to bridge the communication gap using AI-powered sign language translation and a learning dictionary.

## Product Purpose
To provide a premium, accessible, and high-performance tool that translates Thai Sign Language into text/speech and provides a comprehensive dictionary, fostering inclusive communication.

## Brand Personality
Empathetic, Visionary, Premium. The interface should feel like a bridge—reliable yet technically extraordinary.

## Anti-references
- Clinical or medical-looking interfaces.
- Generic SaaS dashboards with "data-slop".
- Overly cluttered UIs with no hierarchy.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity is Empathy**: Every interaction should be clear and intentional.
- **Tech-Forward Inclusivity**: Use high-end visuals to show that accessibility is a first-class citizen.
- **Human-Centric Motion**: Use purposeful motion to guide the user without distraction.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast support.
- Thai language support (Sarabun font).
- Gesture-based interaction optimization.
